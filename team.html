Introduction

We are the team of AmiiboDex, a friendly web application that helps you collect your amiibo of the Super Smash Bros. line! AmiiboDex is made to help users keep track on their collection and even help them to design their own collection list.

Our team culture consists of meeting up on a weekly basis and develop the application together. We are primarily locatied occasionally at the Geisel Library, where we discuss and updated each other on important issues and development.
